Top 10 States with the Most Hate Crime per capita in 2022

Right below you will find a map of the United States, with markers in the states that have the highest hate crime rate. In the cards below that, you will find more description about each state. Each card has the state, the total number of hate crimes, per capita, state population, and what categories the crimes fell into.

United States of America

The United States had a total of 11,613 hate crimes in 2022, according to the Federal Bureau of Investigation. Hate crime is defined by the Oxford Dictionary as "a crime, typically one involving violence, that is motivated by prejudice on the basis of ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, or similar grounds." The United States dictates that if a crime committed has been fueled with prejudice precursors then said offence could be deemed as a hate crime. Hate crimes do not only violate the victim, but also the community to which the victim belongs.

#1 Washington D.C

Washington D.C. has the highest hate crime rate per capita in the U.S. For every 100,000 people in D.C. there are 22.5 hate crimes. D.C. has a population of 671,805. Washington D.C. had 151 hate crimes last year. Of those 151, 71 were racially motivated, 52 were based on sexual orientation, 23 gender identity, 4 based on religion, and 1 on disability.
